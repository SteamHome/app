Suggest links for your favorite games

SteamHome is built to support any game on Steam. However, there are way more than 5,000 games and apps available, and there's no way I could become familiar with each title and its respective community. I've done my best to get SteamHome started with the most popular titles and what seems like the most relevent links for each title.

You guys know more about your favorite game than I can hope to, but that doesn't mean SteamHome can't be uniquely tailored for it. So go ahead, suggest a few of the best resources for your favorite games!
If you're familar with Github, feel free to send in Pull Requests to the gamelinks repo. This is currently the fastest way for me to manage link suggestions
I have plans for a better integrated link suggestion tool, but for now you can suggest links via r/SteamHome or Github
